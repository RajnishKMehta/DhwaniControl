# Quick Settings Tile Guide

Use this walkthrough after opening:
**Home -> Quick Settings Tile -> Config**

## Step 1: Open Quick Settings

1. Swipe down once from the top of the screen.
2. Swipe down again to fully expand Quick Settings.

![Step 1 - Open Quick Settings](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/RajnishKMehta/images/7d3306bbda00f0f5a7650b5810cdae34cd508393/DhwaniControl/quick-tile-step-1.webp)

## Step 2: Enter Edit Mode

1. Tap the edit control (usually a pencil icon or **Edit tiles**).
2. Find **Volume Panel** from *DhwaniControl* in available tiles.

![Step 2 - Enter Edit Mode](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/RajnishKMehta/images/7d3306bbda00f0f5a7650b5810cdae34cd508393/DhwaniControl/quick-tile-step-2.webp)

## Step 3: Add the Tile

1. Drag **Volume Panel** into your active tiles area.
2. Place it where it is easy to reach.

![Step 3 - Add the Tile](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/RajnishKMehta/images/7d3306bbda00f0f5a7650b5810cdae34cd508393/DhwaniControl/quick-tile-step-3.webp)

## Step 4: Save and Test

1. Save the tile layout (or go back if your device auto-saves).
2. Open Quick Settings again.
3. Tap **Volume Panel** and confirm Android volume controls appear.

![Step 4 - Save and Test](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/RajnishKMehta/images/7d3306bbda00f0f5a7650b5810cdae34cd508393/DhwaniControl/quick-tile-step-4.webp)

## Notes

- In-app guide downloads and caches screenshots in app-private storage.
- Screenshot labels and tile edit UI can vary by OEM skin.
