DhwaniControl is an app that helps you control your device volume when the physical volume buttons are broken or not working properly.

The app gives quick access to the native Android volume panel using different methods, so you can adjust volume without relying on hardware keys.

Features:

- Quick Settings Tile to open the volume panel directly from Android Quick Settings
- Floating on-screen button for easy access anywhere
- Edge swipe gesture support to quickly open volume controls
- Uses the native Android system volume panel
- Customization options for the floating button and gestures
- Lightweight and Battery Friendly
- Minimal permissions with no unnecessary access
- No trackers or ads
- Open source and publicly available on GitHub/GitLab

Certain features may ask for additional permissions depending on how you choose to use the app.
