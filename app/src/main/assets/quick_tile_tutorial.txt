Quick Settings Tile Guide

Step 1: Open Quick Settings
- Swipe down from the top of your screen two times.
- Tap the Edit button (pen icon or Edit tiles).
- Screenshot link: https://example.com

Step 2: Add Volume Panel tile
- Find the Volume Panel tile from DhwaniControl.
- Drag it into active tiles and save.
- Screenshot link: https://example.com

Step 3: Use the tile
- Open Quick Settings and tap Volume Panel tile.
- Android volume controls will open instantly.
- Screenshot link: https://example.com
